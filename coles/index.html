Who We Are

A steady hand on Capital Region real estate.

Cole's Capital Group acquires, improves, and holds quality real estate for the long term. We are not flippers chasing a quick exit, we are owners building durable, income-producing assets and the relationships that sustain them.

From residential holdings to commercial space and hospitality, our portfolio reflects a disciplined approach: buy well, manage closely, and create lasting value for partners, tenants, and the communities we invest in.
