Our Story

A little land, looked after.

Pines & Ponies began with a single cabin at the edge of the woods and a simple idea: give people a place that slows them down. Today our small collection spans forest, water, and pasture across the Capital Region and the southern Adirondacks.

Every property is owned and maintained by us, not a faceless management company. That means real local tips, real flexibility, and a stay that actually feels personal.
